01

Who We Are

Kaizen Academy Australia operates the online learning platform at academy.continuousimprovement.education and related websites. We provide continuous improvement, lean, and business process training to individuals and organisations worldwide.

When you use our services, you're trusting us with information about yourself. We take that seriously. This policy explains what we collect, why, and what your rights are.

02

Information We Collect

We only collect what we actually need. That includes:

  • Account information — name, email address, and password when you register for a course or create an account.
  • Payment information — billing details processed securely through our payment provider. We don't store full card numbers.
  • Course activity — progress, quiz results, and completion records so we can issue certificates and track your learning.
  • Communications — messages you send to us, survey responses, and support requests.
  • Technical data — IP address, browser type, device information, and usage patterns collected automatically when you visit our site.
We don't collect sensitive categories of personal data (such as health information, ethnicity, or political views) through our platform.
03

How We Use Your Information

Your information is used to:

  • Deliver courses and issue completion certificates
  • Process payments and send receipts
  • Send course updates, new releases, and promotional offers (you can unsubscribe any time)
  • Respond to support requests
  • Improve our platform and understand how learners use it
  • Meet legal and compliance obligations

We don't use your data for automated decision-making that produces legal or similarly significant effects on you.

04

Sharing Your Information

We don't sell your personal information. We may share data with trusted third parties who help us run the platform — including our learning management system provider, payment processor, and email service provider. All are contractually required to handle your data securely and only for the purpose we've specified.

We may also disclose information if required by law, or to protect the rights, safety, or property of Kaizen Academy Australia or others.

05

Cookies & Tracking

Our platform uses cookies to keep you logged in, remember your preferences, and understand how people use the site. We may also use analytics tools (such as Google Analytics) that set their own cookies.

You can control cookies through your browser settings. Disabling them may affect some functionality — particularly logging in and saving your course progress.

06

Data Retention

We keep your account and course data for as long as your account is active, or as needed to provide services. If you request deletion, we'll remove your personal data within a reasonable timeframe, unless we're required to retain it for legal or compliance purposes (such as financial records).

Completion records may be retained longer to support certificate verification.

07

Your Rights

Depending on where you're located, you may have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Correct inaccurate or incomplete data
  • Request deletion of your personal data
  • Opt out of marketing communications
  • Data portability — receive a copy of your data in a common format
  • Object to certain types of processing

To exercise any of these rights, get in touch using the contact details below. We'll respond within 30 days.

08

Security

We use industry-standard measures to protect your data — including encrypted connections (HTTPS), secure payment processing, and access controls. No method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure, but we take reasonable precautions to protect your information.

If you suspect unauthorised access to your account, please contact us immediately.

09

Third-Party Links

Our platform may contain links to external websites. We're not responsible for the privacy practices of those sites and recommend reviewing their policies separately.

10

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. When we do, we'll revise the "last updated" date at the top of the page. For significant changes, we'll notify you by email or through a notice on the platform.

Continuing to use our services after changes take effect means you accept the updated policy.
